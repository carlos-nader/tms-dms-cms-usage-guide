TMS/DMS/CMS Usage Guide

F-16 HOTAS Switches Reference for Falcon BMS 4.38.1

About This Guide

This comprehensive guide covers the Target Management Switch (TMS), Display Management Switch (DMS), and Countermeasures Management Switch (CMS) functions in the F-16 Fighting Falcon combat flight simulator (Falcon BMS 4.38.1 and previous versions). These HOTAS (Hands On Throttle And Stick) switches are essential for sensor management, targeting, navigation, and defensive systems control during realistic combat missions.

The guide provides detailed tables and explanations for switch functions across all master modes (NAV, A-A, A-G, DGFT, MSL Override), sensor of interest (SOI) management, MFD interactions, radar operations, target management and countermeasures deployment. Whether you're a beginner learning basic HOTAS operations or an experienced simmer mastering advanced tactical employment, this reference will help you efficiently manage F-16 systems without taking your hands off the controls.
